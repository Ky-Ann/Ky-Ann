My analysis of the most useful information.

Research suggests that a healthy adult should sleep between 7-9 hours (420-540 minutes) a night. Sleep Research Most people fall within this range. However, the data outside of this average shows that we can market to those that are struggling with sleep.

Conclusion

Three key pieces of information came from this data to help our marketing division.

1:We can market our device for people upset about their malfunctioning smart devices.

Smart devices being used seem to malfunction quite a bit. People are burning a lot of calories with 0 steps being counted. We can market to the customers that believe their devices are faulty and not accurately displaying their activity.

2:Our product can help people keep track of their sleepless nights.

Sleep is usually an important factor in people’s lives, and overall effect’s one’s health. We can market to those that are having trouble with using our product to help them track it.

3:Our Fitbit is not only for high achieving athletes.

Most steps were recorded during a light active distance. As well as the average being 7637 steps. A bit below the 10,000 a day goal. Thus, showing that you do not need to be an extreme athlete to use a smart device. It can be used for any goal, by anyone.
